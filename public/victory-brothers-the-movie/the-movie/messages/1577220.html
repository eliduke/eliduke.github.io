I'm no peyton

Posted by Tyler Beem on 22 Jan, 2006
Category: the story

so people, although i have not as of yet posted part of the script, rest assured, i am writing it. i’ve been chipping away at it for the past few weeks (also drew just dropped off my keys, and i think all of you are fags)

anyways, what i need, or would like to have at least is suggestions for character traits that your characters will have in the show. hit me back with some of your thoughts, otherwise i will just create them myself and you may not like it (eli goes hogging all the time)

examples of what i’m looking for include the overall demeener, are they calm, excited nervous? what do they hate? what do they love? Also think about how you can demonstrate that your character goes a little bit deeper. i.e. do they have a soft spot for the homeless? or what else can elicit a deep, real emotion. its important that the characters feel three demontional. they also need to do some changing as the show goes along.

i promise, as soon as i get a rough draft of the first part done, i’ll get it posted for revision.

lets not be peyton mannings. it does us no good to have all the skill in the world only to not finish what we started.

tyler

About Us

Roseburg Saw & Tool Company was originally founded as K & S Precision Grinding in 2002 by Dan Lines. When starting this company, Dan had twenty-five years of sawfiling experience in both primary and secondary manufacturing in a variety of wood species.

Our beginning focus was on the sawfiling industry. We provided a source of hand crafted saw hammers, tension gauges, and straight edges.

In 2006, the company relocated to Roseburg, Oregon and began to include service work for the wood products industry in Douglas County. In 2010, K & S Precision Grinding was renamed Roseburg Saw & Tool Company in an effort to better reflecting the services we provide.

Saw Hammers

Crossface

Crossface hammers are primarily used for bandsaws. They are composed of two elongated faces, one parallel to the handle and the other perpendicular.

Dog Head

Dog Head hammers are primarily used for circular saws. They have a tapered head and the handle is oriented on the narrow end of the head. There is only one circular shape striking face.

Combination

Combination hammers are commonly used for both band and circular saws. There's one elongated face parallel to the handle and the other face is circular.

Twist

Twist hammers are typically used for circular saws. The Twist hammer is composed of two elongated faces. They are both 45 degrees to the handle, one right and the other left.

Tension Gauges

These gauges are used in the benching process for bandsaws. They are straight on one edge and convex on the other. The convex edge, or tension edge, is ground to the customer's specified foot-circle.

Straight Edges

Straight edges are primarily used in the benching and hammering process for circular saws. These straight edges are a "roof-top" style with only one ground straight edge.

Speed Gauges

Speed Gauges are designed to measure the amount of tension in large circular saws based on their RPM. These gauges are convex on one edge and concave on the other.

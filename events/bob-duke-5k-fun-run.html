Here We Go Again!

That's right all you non-Duke Kids... it's time for the 4th Annual All Non-Duke Kids Bob Duke 5k Memorial Fun Run! It has become such a wonderful springtime tradition for us, and we would love for you to join in the celebration. :)

Even though it's called a fun "run", running is not the only option! You are more than welcome to run, walk, bike, blade, skate, scoot, or whatever else your heart can dream up.

We'll be providing pizza, dark beer, and blueberries for refreshments after the race is over.

WHO?

YOU along with any family or friends you want to include. THE MOST IMPORTANT PART RIGHT NOW is that *we need you to RSVP with t-shirt sizes* so we know how many of each size to order. You can message any of the Duke Kids and we'll add you to the list.

08: Sunsets and Snow

The sunsets around here last for hours, and it's absolutely delicious. They go on and on and on, getting better and better with age. Sometimes I just have to walk away and go to bed because they really don't ever end in the same way that they do back home.

{% include warning.html %}

I love the contrast of the wide open sunset sky with the cozy tucked in snowy and cloudy days and nights.

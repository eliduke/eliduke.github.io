02: The Nathaniel B Palmer

At 9am the following morning they pulled up the gangway, tossed the lines, and pushed away from the dock: we were now officially underway to Palmer Station! Very exciting stuff. The energy was high, our crew was excited to finally be on our way, but there was also a bit of a somber element in the air: this would be the final voyage to Antarctica for the Nathanial B Palmer after 33 years of service with the National Science Foundation.

The sunset that first night knocked my socks off.

And we got to hang out on the bridge! The crew was super chill.

The butterfly loop, also known as lineman's loop, butterfly knot, alpine butterfly knot and lineman's rider, is a knot used to form a fixed loop in the middle of a rope. Tied in the bight, it can be made in a rope without access to either of the ends; this is a distinct advantage when working with long climbing ropes for example. . There are several ways to tie this knot, but in this example the method is: Twist the line once to form a large loop, and again to form a second smaller loop.
